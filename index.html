Frequently asked

The questions we get on every intro call.

What does "Fortune 5000" PM really mean here? +

PMs must have at least 5 years senior-level tenure at a Fortune-5000-grade builder, developer, or owner's-rep firm (Bechtel, Suffolk, Turner, Lürssen, Aman, MGM, etc.), and a portfolio of two finished programs at $10M+. We verify with W-2s, project records, and reference calls.

How are rates set? +

PMs propose, we benchmark against tenure and asset class, then post a single locked rate. Hourly today is $260–$465/hr. Full-program engagements can be flat-fee, typically 1–2% of construction value.

How do you protect athlete privacy? +

All PMs sign a strict NDA before they see the address. Site visits run under a project codename. Photography is opt-in. We've never had a leak.

What does Tier1 charge clients? +

10% platform fee on top of PM rates — covering escrow, NDA/MSA paperwork, senior reviewer on every program, and a swap guarantee if the fit isn't right.

What happens if the PM isn't a fit? +

Flag it in the first 80 hours and we'll swap the PM or refund the work — your call. After that, escrowed milestones protect both sides.
