Frequently asked

The questions we get on every intro call.

What does "Fortune 500" really mean here? +

Operators must have at least 3 years of full-time tenure at a current or recent Fortune 500 company in a finance, accounting, treasury, or strategic-finance function. We verify with W-2s, badge emails, or reference calls — not just LinkedIn.

How are rates set? +

Operators propose, we benchmark against tenure and specialty, and post a single locked rate. No bidding, no haggling. Range today is $180–$340/hr. Projects can be priced as flat fees.

What does Tier1 charge clients? +

We add a 10% platform fee on top of the operator's rate — covering escrow, NDA/MSA paperwork, a fractional-CFO reviewer on every engagement, and a swap guarantee if it isn't working.

Can operators stay anonymous to their employer? +

Yes. Profiles are masked by default — clients see first name + initial, tenure, and specialty. Full identity unlocks only after the operator accepts a brief.

What happens if the engagement isn't working? +

Flag it in the first 10 hours and we'll swap the operator or refund the work — your call. After that, escrowed milestones protect both sides.
